Process

Three apps, three stages

Step 1

Trace in RoughAnimator

Each pose is hand-traced on mobile using onion-skinning for consistency.

Step 2

Composite in After Effects

Frame sequences are cleaned up, colored, and timed for full clips.

Step 3

Edit & export in CapCut

Final clips are trimmed, synced to music, and exported on mobile.
