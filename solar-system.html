3D Solar System

A 3D scene that explores planetary scale, lighting, orbit-inspired composition, and the visual storytelling of outer space.

Concept

A cinematic view of planets and motion.

The solar system project turns astronomical structure into a stylized visual environment. The focus is on spatial depth, glowing light sources, and arranging planets so the scene feels both educational and visually striking.

Production Notes

Key design decisions

  • Use sphere-based modeling for planets and supporting rings.
  • Apply texture and material settings to distinguish surfaces.
  • Position lights to create contrast, highlights, and mood.
  • Organize the scene for camera-friendly composition and animation.
