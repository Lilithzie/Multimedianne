Apps Used

Four apps were used in this portfolio. Rotoscoping was done on mobile with RoughAnimator, polished in CapCut, composited in Adobe After Effects, and the solar system project was built in Blender.

Rotoscope (Mobile)

RoughAnimator

Used on mobile to trace frames and build the core rotoscope animation sequence.

Rotoscope (Mobile)

CapCut

Used to edit, time, and export the final rotoscope output on mobile.

Compositing

Adobe After Effects

Used for compositing, timing polish, and finishing touches on the rotoscope sequences.

Solar System

Blender

Used to model, texture, light, and render the 3D solar system project.
